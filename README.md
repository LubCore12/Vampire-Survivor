A simple 2D survival game built with **Python** and **Pygame**. Your

## üéÆ Gameplay

You play as a survivor equipped with a gun. Monsters continuously spawn
and try to defeat you. There is no ending - only survival. Try to beat
your best time and stay alive as long as you can.

Difficulty increases over time: - More monsters spawn - Enemies become
faster - The game becomes more intense and challenging
